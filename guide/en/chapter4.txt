A chapter is the lowest unit on the Course hierarchy and it consists in a page. Every chapter has its own title and content.

== Add a chapter ==
To add a chapter into a section, go the course main page. Find the section where you want to add the chapter and click on the <code>edit section</code>link. Write the name of the chapter on the input field and save the changes by clicking on the<code>save section</code>button.
