WikiToLearn is actively developed both on the technical and the content side.
It's possible to contribute to WikiToLearn in several ways, regardless of the user's skills and experience.

== Software development ==
There are lots of app developed by WikiToLearn where you can be useful.
Web development is split into groups and every group has its maintainer.
Take a look at the [http://meta.wikitolearn.org/Tech_Team Tech Team community page] and feel free to contact the members to ask them more questions.

== Translation ==
If you want to help with translating you can join the [https://chat.wikitolearn.org/channel/translation #translation channel].

== Bug tracking ==
We use phabricator to keep tracks of our development and our bugs.
If you think you have found a bug and want to tell us about it, you need to [https://identity.kde.org/index.php?r=registration/index register to KDE identity], log in to Phabricator and   [https://phabricator.kde.org/maniphest/task/edit/form/1/?tags=WikiToLearn create a ticket].
