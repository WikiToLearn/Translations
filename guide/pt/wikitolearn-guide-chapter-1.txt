O WikiToLearn é um portal que pretende criar livros de texto livres, gratuitos, colaborativos e acessíveis imediatamente.
A nossa filosofia pode-se resumir como "''O conhecimento só cresce se for partilhado''".

== Convenções ==
O WikiToLearn tira um uso abrangente de um modelo hierárquico, para poder criar livros que sejam consistentes com a nossa filosofia.

=== Departamentos ===
It's a collection of topics, courses and books belonging to the same area.
Departamentos are listed on the main page.

=== Tópicos ===
Tópicos are the level underneath Departamentos and contain different courses related to the same subject.
They are used to gather all the courses that explain the same theme, because every university and faculty could have a different approach to the subject.
For example, <code>Quantum Physics</code> may be a topic and <code>Quantum Mechanics for graduate students</code>, <code>Advanced Quantum Mechanics</code> may be a course.

[[File:Topic_example_wtl.png]]

=== Cursos ===
Cursos are the most important units of WikiToLearn.
Cursos are collections of chapters, which gather the pages (sections).
Every course has the prefix <code>Course:</code> followed by the course name in its URL.

[[File:Course_example_wtl.png]]

=== Capítulos ===
Capítulos are the lower level of a course.
They gather together some sections of a book and they are displayed as headings in the main page of the course.

[[File:Chapter_example_wtl.png]]

=== Secções ===
Secções are the basic unit of an entire course.
Basically, they're a single webpage with its own content.

[[File:Section_example_wtl.png]]

== Espaços de nomes ==
Espaços de nomes are a mechanism for grouping pages.
You can see the namespace by looking at the url: if it has a <code>word:something_else</code>then "word" is the namespace.
In WikiTolearn there are two main namespaces, "User" and "Course".

=== Espaço de nomes do utilizador ===
The Espaço de nomes do utilizador is your personal page where you can write your own courses and content without worrying about making mistakes.
You can see an example here:

http://en.wikitolearn.org/User:Grigoletti

=== Espaço de nomes do curso ===
A Espaço de nomes do curso is similar to the User one, but it has the word <code>Course:</code>as a prefix to the URL<ref>URL is an acronym for Uniform Resource Locator and is a reference (an address) to a resource on the Internet</ref>.
This namespace is used to create an entire public course.
You can see an example on [[Course:How to use Linked Data|How to use Linked Data]]!
